## SSSD (NSS, PAM, ...)

* <https://docs.pagure.org/SSSD.sssd/>

* Open Source Client for Enterprise Identity Management.

* Enroll your Linux machine into an Active Directory, FreeIPA or LDAP domain. 
  Use remote identities, policies and various authentication and authorization 
  mechanisms to access your computer. [[identity.MS_Entra,identity.FreeIPA,identity.LDAP]]

* system daemon providing access to local or remote identity and
  authentication resources through a common framework that can
  provide caching and offline support to the system. 

* It provides several interfaces, including NSS and PAM modules or
  a D-Bus interface.
